### Data
These data used by this tool was gathered by students from the UCI: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones. The data was generated by group of 30 volunteers within an age bracket of 19-48 years. Each person performed six activities (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING) wearing a smartphone (Samsung Galaxy S II) on the waist.
